Ramen

Ramen is the GOAT

Ramen is typically a Japanese noodle soup.It consists of a combination of a rich flavoured broth, one varity of a type of noodle and a selection of meats,greens or vegetables, often topped with a boiled egg.

Ramen is well known throughout the world and is loved by many. There are many different variations of ramen. Ramen is heavily regionalised throughtout Japab so different areas of Japan would have different and unqiue styles and flavours of this delicious dish.

Ingredients

  • 8 cups tonkotsu pork broth
  • 12 oz good quality dried ramen noodles
  • thinly sliced green onions
    • large or extra large eggs
  • 2-3 oz enoki or other mushrooms
  • Chashu pork belly
  • Soy bacon tare/Miso Tare

    • Recipe

    1. Boil the ramen noodles in plenty of water as directed by the packaging.
    2. Cook the mushrooms until softened along side the noodles.
    3. Fry the Chashu pork until lightly browned.
    4. Place 1/4 of whichever tare you are using in the bottom of 4 bowls.
    5. Carefully Ladle a 1/2 cup of the tonkotsu broth into each bowl and mix.
    6. Add the noodles into the bowls and then pour in 1 1/2 cups of the tonkotsu broth over it.
    7. Top with a medium boilded egg, mushrooms, pork and green onions
