Karaage Chicken

Karaage Chicken is the Bomb!

A beautiful marinated piece of chicken mixed with soy sauce, sake, ginger and mirin to give a touch of sweetness.

The extremely popular Japanese dish is deep fried twice to make them succulent, juicy and most importantly crunchy.

Ingredients

  • Chicken thigh fillets cut into large bite-size pieces
  • Corn flour/cornstarch
  • Vegetable oil for deep-frying
  • Marinade (soy sauce, cooking sake, mirin, freshly grated ginger including juice)

Recipe

  1. Cut the fillets into large bite size pieces
  2. Put the chicken pieces and the Marinade ingredients in a ziplock bag, and massage well to coat every piece of meat with the marinade
  3. Marinate for 30 minutes to 1 hour.
  4. Pat-dry the chicken pieces.
  5. Coat the chicken pieces with corn flour/cornstarch.
