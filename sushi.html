Sushi

SUSHI TIME BABY!!!

Sushi needs no explanation.

If you don't know what sushi is, you're living under a rock. If you also don't like sushi, you should rethink your life choices.

Ingredients

  • 6 sheets sushi seaweed
  • 1 batch prepared sushi rice
  • 1/2 lb sushi-grade raw salmon or desired raw fish of choice
  • 4 oz cream cheese sliced into strip
  • 1 avocado sliced
  • soy sauce for serving

Recipe

  1. Lace the seaweed on a bamboo mat, then cover the sheet of seaweed with an even layer of prepared sushi rice. Smooth gently with the rice paddle.
  2. Layer salmon, cream cheese, and avocado on the rice, and roll it up tightly. Slice with a sharp knife, and enjoy with soy sauce.
  3. Enjoy!
